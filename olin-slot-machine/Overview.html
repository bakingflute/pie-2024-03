OVERVIEW

PROJECT OBJECTIVES

For this project, we created an old-fashioned mechanical slot machine. Our objectives were for our slot machine to be coin-activated with jackpots that are predetermined (much like real slot machines, the odds are never in the player's favor).

Mechanical

Build a life-sized frame for the slot machine

Wheels and gears that spin smoothly

Olin-themed and designed to engage the community

Electrical

Integrate motors, sensors, LEDs, and speakers to smoothly display jackpot effects

Have a coin dispensing mechanism that can dispense different number of coins

Software

Develop software to manage the odds of the game, 


Predetermine outcomes so that jackpots are deterministic
