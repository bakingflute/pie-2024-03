Fred the Fortune Telling Frog is an animatronic machine that tells your fortune with the press of a button. From his immersive pond environment, you will be entranced by his twinkling lights, glowing flower, and inviting eyes. Once you press the button, he comes alive with his head moving in a pan-tilt mechanism, the arms moving up and down, and the torso moving side-to-side. Fred will kindly ask you how you are doing and pick up on the sentiment of what you say.  If he senses a negative tone to your answer, he will cater his fortune to resonate with someone who is feeling down. If he senses a positive tone, he will provide a fortune that resonates with someone who is already having a good day. Fred is our modernized rendition of the classic Zoltar fortune telling machine, and is an amalgamation of mechanical, software, electrical, and aesthetic contributions from our team.
