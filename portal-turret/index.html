AABC PORTAL TURRET

A replica of the turret from the video game Portal.

Our goal was to create a replica of the Turret from the video game Portal, which can move in response to external stimuli using motors and sensors, play music and sounds, and cycle through different modes.


The Turret would need to perform the basic functions of:
  1. Opening and closing wings
  2. Playing and responding to music
  3. Reacting when picked up
